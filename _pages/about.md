My name is Jared, I am second year master's student at Brown University 🐻

My research interests are in cosmology, theoretical astrophysics, and statistical mechanics. My background spans Cosmic Mircowave Background (CMB) experiment, high energy theory, compuation, and data analytics. I also teach everything from middle school mathematics to quantum theory. 

My master's thesis can be found in the Research tab. In this work, I performed a comparative analysis of primary beam models for the Murchison Widefield Array. I was able to modify a class of beam emulators to reduce model evaluation time by a factor > 3, helping the emulators run faster than prior implementations.
